# to-the-moon-mars-and-beyond
Can you make it to Mars?
You are the pilot of Elon Musk’s next-gen Starship on a daring high-speed journey to Mars. Fly horizontally across the solar system while a deadly asteroid field grows more intense by the second.
You have only one life and exactly 2 minutes to survive. The longer you last, the faster the ship goes and the more asteroids appear.
Tap or press SPACE to boost upward. Release to fall. Master the timing, thread the needle between rocks, and keep your Starship in one piece until you finally see the red planet on the horizon.
